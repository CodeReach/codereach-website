CodeReach teaches the fundementals of computer science to Grade 7 and 8 students

CodeReach helps create the foundation of computer science for students to prepare them for more complicated computer science classes in the future.

Our Goal

In an ever growing technology-driven world, we strive to prepare young minds in our community for what the future holds. Programming is a sought-after skill that, unfortunately, is not taught in elementary school. We hope that through programming, our students will be proactive when using computers and hopefully reach a point where they can make whatever they want.

What we teach

We teach Python because its simple to learn and is one of the most popular programming languages in the world. Further into the course, we introduce students to basic graphics using the module, Pygame. Students will have the opportunity to learn how to create their own simple games.

Who teaches

At CodeReach, our instructors are highly qualified highschool students. All instructors must finish their computer science and math course with over a 95%. We hope that through a peer to peer setting, classrooms can become more dynamic and less intimidating!

Frequently Asked Questions

If the section below doesn't answer your question, feel free to use the contact form below.

How much does CodeReach cost?

CodeReach is free. We believe that open access to education is extremely important, so we will never charge for CodeReach.

What should I bring to class?

We provide computer labs, so you do not have to bring your own computer. That being said we highly recommend you bring your own laptop so you can continue on what you learned in class at home! If you have any medical issues, allergies or otherwise require accommodation, please let us know! We also suggest you bring water in a bottle so you don't get thirsty.

How do I apply?

Due to limited number of seats, we offer CodeReach at a first come first serve basis. To apply click the “signup” button at the start of the page. You can’t miss it we promise!

When are applications due? How will I know if I got accepted?

Information will be released soon.

Applications are due by September 10th, 2018. We will send out an email to all accepted students by September 15th, 2018.
